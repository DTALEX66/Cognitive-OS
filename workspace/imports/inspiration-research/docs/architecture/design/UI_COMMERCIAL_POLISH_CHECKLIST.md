# UI Commercial Polish Checklist

## Visual principles

- Top navigation remains simple and functional.
- Page titles may use poetic names, but each page also shows the plain functional title.
- Important actions are always visible in the page header or primary card.
- Risk and trust information must be visible before route copying or publishing.
- Empty states must tell the user what to do next.

## Required UI modules

- Commercial readiness panel.
- Route timeline preview.
- Route trust panel.
- Empty state.
- Risk notice.
- Quality bars.
- Route package import/export tools.

## Commercial demo criteria

- The home page explains the product in one screen.
- The five-step learning flow is visible.
- The route asset workspace is understandable without documentation.
- Community copy actions show source/change/audit context.
- The quality gate is discoverable from settings and sidebar.
