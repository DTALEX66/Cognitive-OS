# Brand Execution Guidelines

## Product Tone

Lumina is a quiet technical companion for learning and knowledge operations. Copy should be direct, useful, and calm. Avoid hype language and decorative claims.

## Visual Tone

- Materials: white, graphite, soft blue, green status, amber risk.
- Shape: small radii for operational panels, larger radii only for hero app frames.
- Iconography: Lucide icons for commands and workflow states.
- Motion: functional, not ornamental.

## Naming

Use Lumina KB for the product shell and Knowledge Base for the repository/system context.
